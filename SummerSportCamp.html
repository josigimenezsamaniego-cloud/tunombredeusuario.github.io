About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of three young, sport-passionate students from Madrid who believe that the best summers are active ones. We created SummerSport Camp because we wanted to offer children and teenagers a safe, fun and professional place to practise sport during the school holidays.

Our main goal is simple: to help every child improve their physical skills, build confidence and make new friends — all while having the time of their lives.

You can trust us because our sessions are led by certified coaches, our groups are small (maximum 15 kids), and we always put safety and fun first.

What we offer

Our Sports & Activities

Choose from five exciting sports. Morning and afternoon sessions available for all age groups: 6–10, 11–14 and 15–17 years.

Team sport

Football

Technique, tactics and team play. Drills and mini-matches every day. Suitable for all levels from beginner to advanced.

Team sport

Basketball

Dribbling, shooting and defensive skills. We finish each session with a full 5v5 game. High energy and great fun.

Individual

Swimming

From basic strokes to competitive technique. All sessions held in a supervised indoor pool with certified instructors.

Mixed

Multi-sport days

Try volleyball, athletics, tennis and more. Perfect for kids who want to explore different sports before specialising.
